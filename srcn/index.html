We Are Santhiram College of Nursing

Santhiram College of Nursing was founded in 2008 by the Shafa Educational Society, an esteemed non-profit organization established in 2001 that is committed to advancing education and healthcare in the region.
The College is recognized by the Government of AP, APNMC, and approved by the Indian Nursing Council (INC) New Delhi. And affiliated by the Dr. NTR University Health Sciences, Vijayawada. Intake seats 100 Students for every year.
Located in Nandyal, a charming town in the Nandyal district of Andhra Pradesh, Santhiram College of Nursing Campus sits on NH-40 and enjoys excellent connectivity through road, rail, and air. Under the auspices of the Shafa Educational Association, Santhiram College of Nursing was founded with community development.
Additionally, SRCN is dedicated to offering all high-quality medical treatment at low costs. The concept of a medical school in a remote area can grow and produce competent physicians. Medical administration and support personnel at Santhiram General Hospital in our Campus are highly trained and committed to providing excellent care and service to the community at all times.

Our Facilities

Santhiram Medical College has a sprawling campus spread over an area of 110 acres.

Girls' Hostel:

SRCN girls’ hostel is known for safety and security to the non residential students who want to study Nursing in a home like environment. Besides the facilities like spacious rooms, Wi-Fi, internet facility, and refrigerators supplying mineral water, SRCN girls’ hostel takes a special care on girl students such as acting strict towards allowing them for outings; keeping eye during the nights; providing counselling to the students who are found home sick; medicating the students who are suffering from ill health.

Facilities at Hostel

Santhiram College of Nursing provides excellent Hostel Facility for Girls as well as Girls individually. Spacious rooms with attached bathrooms are available. Well connected to the transport area; excellent facilities for dining and sports areas are the most attractive things to the students.

Faculty Quarters

The faculty from other states and regions who would like to stay and work for SRCN are given separate faculty quarters with all basic amenities. SRCN has double bedroom individual flats for accommodating faculty from outside of AP. The staff quarters are very near to the campus, and it is adjacent to students’ hostel. The quarters are facilitated with the power, internet, and water 24 X 7.

Following facilities are being provided in the Hostels:
  1. Well spacious, hygiene, secured and Uninterrupted Power Supply rooms.
  2. Wi-Fi Enabled rooms.
  3. Banking Facility of BOI along with ATM centre
  4. Medical facility: free medicines are provided to the students who feels illness
  5. Medical Lab facility is available.
  6. Mineral water is provided.
  7. Sweets and Juice centre available for students after college hours
  8. Facilities to play Indoor and Outdoor Games
  9. Laundry and Ironing facility are also available for free of cost.
