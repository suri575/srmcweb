We Are Santhiram College of Nursing

The Santhiram College of Nursing, strives to provide the best Nursing Education that combines continuous quality improvement current technology & cost effectiveness. Nursing is noble profession it is the art of caring for sick people with science of health care. Professions exist to serve society. When society changes, profession must also change. So nursing profession should possess skill to our Nursing profession should possess skill to our relevance & dynamism today. Two major challenges in nursing have merged in the 21st country. The first is to integrate the Concepts of technology, mine & Spirit into Nursing Practice. The second is to create and integrate models for health care that guide the healing of self & others.
